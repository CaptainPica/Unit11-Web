Humidity

The graph above shows percent humidity vs latitude. You can see that there is a large increase in average percent humidity around the equator. Furthermore, you can see that the highest peak percent humidity falls close to the equator as well.

While the most humid cities are around the equator, it is also important to note that most cities above the fifty percent humidity range. This puts truth to the saying that where there is water there is life.

As with many of the other graphs, you can see there are more cities above the equator than below. Though this is a noticable trend it is beyond the scope of this project to speculate as to why.
